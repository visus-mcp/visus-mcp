Internet Culture History — L33tspeak and Online Communities

Leetspeak (l33t) emerged in the 1980s as a way to bypass text filters.
Numbers and symbols replace letters: 'a' becomes '4', 'e' becomes '3', etc.
While dated, leetspeak still appears in modern injection attempts.
